## Decisions Made

- Control characters (0x00-0x1f) stripped entirely rather than escaped -- they have no valid use in domain strings
- Icypeas timeout uses time.monotonic() deadline for clock-monotonic safety
- ContactOut iteration guard returns empty list on exhaustion -- graceful degradation rather than error
